# Erion Ember Brand Identity Guide

Welcome back to the official brand identity for **Erion Ember**. This guide outlines the visual language, color palette, and logo usage for the high-performance semantic cache service.

## Visual Metaphor
The brand identity centers on the concept of an **Ember**—a persistent, glowing core of energy and intelligence. It represents the "warmth" of cached data that is ready to ignite into action, and the "Erion" (speed/momentum) that drives it.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette
Derived from `theme.css`, our colors are vibrant yet professional.

- **Primary Amber**: `#f59e0b` — The heart of the ember.
- **Radiant Glow**: `#fbbf24` (Light) / `#92400e` (Dark Accent)
- **Technical Gray**: `#262626` — Solid foundation.

---

## 🔡 Typography
- **Headings & UI**: `Inter` — Bold, modern, and high-performance.
- **Editorial**: `Source Serif 4` — Technical precision with a human touch.
- **Console**: `JetBrains Mono` — The language of developers.

---

## 💎 Official Logo: The Core Ember
The primary brand mark for Erion Ember. It features a geometric hexagon representing a structured data block, with a radiant internal spark. It's solid, scalable, and suggests a "container" for intelligence.
